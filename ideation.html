DOUBT ROOM

Introduction

In a classroom teacher may not clarify each and every student doubts, this website will provide online room for students who belong to the particular section or class. Students can post their doubts in this room once the class ends and then get clarified by belonging teacher and the classmates.


This benifits students who are in schools and colleges.

Expected List of Features

    1. One should able to create portal or room and set a password and id to enter the room
    2. A person should be able to Post the answer and also attach images and text files to it.
    3. A person should be able give ratings to a particular answer
